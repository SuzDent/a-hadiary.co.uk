04       Magne Furuholmen – Kulturkirken Jakob, Oslo, Norway. Magne Magne performs a 1-hour long exclusive "White Xmas Lies" concert at Kulturkirken Jakob in Oslo. Magne and others hand out soup and bread before the concert which starts at 13:00. Magne has an 8-piece band on stage with him (Karl Oluf Wennerberg, Even Ormestad, Morten Qvenild, Tini Flaat Mykland, Madeleine Ossum, Emilie Heldal Lidsheim, Ingvild Nesdal Sandnes and Dan Sunhordvik). A second private show takes place at 18:00.
