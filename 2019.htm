December 2019


01       Magne Furuholmen – Universitetsplassen, Oslo, Norway. Magne peforms one song "There Goes Another Year" at the lighting of the Christmas tree at University Square in Oslo. Magen is guest on NRK P1’s "Nitimen". on the same day, which is available at nrk.no (starts at 28:30).
04       Magne Furuholmen – Kulturkirken Jakob, Oslo, Norway. Magne Magne performs a 1-hour long exclusive "White Xmas Lies" concert at Kulturkirken Jakob in Oslo. Magne and others hand out soup and bread before the concert which starts at 13:00. Magne has an 8-piece band on stage with him (Karl Oluf Wennerberg, Even Ormestad, Morten Qvenild, Tini Flaat Mykland, Madeleine Ossum, Emilie Heldal Lidsheim, Ingvild Nesdal Sandnes and Dan Sunhordvik). A horn section from the Salvation Army joins for the last song: Snow is Falling / The Light We Lost / A Wintry Silence / The Season to be Melancholy / Caprice Des Dieux / This is Now America / You Can’t Have it Both Ways (performed by Tini) / So Cold It’s Hard to Think / White Xmas Lies / Revelation Song / There Goes Another Year.
A second private show takes place at 18:00.
