2003 - How can they sleep

January 2003

1       a-ha's live performance of "Hunting High And Low" is broadcast on "TOTP2" (BBC2) in the UK.
31       "Absolutt Underholdning" (TV2) reports on Morten at the premiere of "Die Another Day".

February 2003

22       Morten presents an award to "Gåte" an the annual Spellemannprisen in Oslo.

March 2003

10       a-ha release a live version of "The Sun Always Shines On TV", taken from the live album "How Can I Sleep With Your Voice In My Head", in Norway and other parts of Europe.

 

24       a-ha release the live album "How Can I Sleep With Your Voice In My Head", in Norway and other parts of Europe.

28       Morten is guest on the Norwegian TV show "Førat og Sist" (NRK1).

April 2003

5       a-ha perform a play-back version of "The Sun Always Shines On TV (live)" and "Summer Moved On" on "Formula Eins" (RTL). Paul is in the States, so a Paul 'double' is brought in for the show.
15       a-ha are on "Generacion Triunfo" (TVE Internacional) in Spain.
19       A four page article on Morten is printed in the Norwegian newspaper VG who followed Morten for three days and went to his new, self designed, working apartment in Oslo and his cabin on an island outside Kristiansand.
20       Morten's baby daughter is christened Henny.

May 2003

9       Morten travels with TV-producer Einar Matre and people from the charity organisation "Strømmestiftelsen" to East Timor. A TV special "Et land i støpeskjeen" ("A country in the making") on this trip will be broadcast on TV2 in Norway on 9 June. The show is produced by TV-Inter.
21-30 November       Magne is exhibiting glass paintings and large glass vases at Cisternerne (Museum of Modern Glass Art) which is below Frederiksberg Castle in Copenagen, Denmark. The jars were made at Hadeland Glassverk in Norway.

August 2003

18       The Norwegian show "TV-Sør nyhetene" includes a report on Morten singing with Harket Mannssanggruppe.

September 2003

19       An interview with Morten in London's Covent Garden is broadcast on the Norwegian TV show "Absolutt Underholdning" (TV2).
20       An interview with Morten and Magne on "Valen TV" is broadcast on the Norwegian TV show "God Kveld Norge" (TV2).
23       a-ha's "Top Of The Pops" performance of "Crying In The Rain" from 18 October 1990 is broadcast on "TOTP2" (BBC2) in the UK.

October 2003

19       Magne and Nico are on TV-aksjonen where the wooden sculpture "Ungt menneske" ("Young human being") is auctioned.
26       The Norwegian TV show "Nyhetene" (TV2) has a report from Novosibirsk on how the city is making preparations for the forthcoming a-ha concert.
27       Sports Palace, Novosibirsk, Siberia The Norwegian TV show "Absolutt Underholdning" (TV2) has a report from Novosibirsk with a clips of: a-ha arriving, press conference and of "Forever Not Yours" from the concert.
28       Concert Hall Ukraina, Kiev, Ukraine
29       Sports Palace, Odessa, Ukraine
31       Ice Arena, St.Petersburg, Russia

November 2003

An interview with Morten on the future of a-ha and family life is broadcast on "God Kveld Norge" (TV2).
2       Kremlin Palace, Moscow, Russia

Jan Werner Danielson releases his album "Singer Of Songs" which includes the track "I Smile When I'm Dreaming", the melody to which was written by Magne and Kjetil.
