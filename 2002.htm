2002 - Forever Not Yours

March 2002

1       Frode receives an award on behalf of Savoy for "Best pop group" at the annual "Spellemannprisen 2001" (NRK1).
29       The Pakistani band "Junoon" release their album "Daur-e-Junoon", it features the track "Piya" sung by Morten Harket.
30       An long interview with Morten and Sondre Lerche is published in the Norwegian newspaper "VG".

The video for "Forever Not Yours" is filmed in Havana, Cuba and directed by Harald Zwart.

April 2002
