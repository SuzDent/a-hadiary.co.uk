March 2016

6       a-ha are back on tour to promote their album "Cast In Steel". The band joining them on tour are Karl Oluf Wennerberg on drums, Erik Ljunggren on synth/programming and bassist Even Ormestad. The Set list has changed slightly since South America with "Giving Up The Ghost" as the opener and the inclusion of "Mother Nature Goes To Heaven" and "Living At The End Of The World". The set list on this first night at at KRK Uralets Arena, Yekaterinburg, Russia is: "Giving Up The Ghost", "I've Been Losing You", "Move To Memphis" "The Swing Of Things", "Stay On These Roads", "Sycamore Leaves", "Mother Nature Goes To Heaven", "Living At The End Of The World", "Crying In The Rain", "Velvet" (Morten & Tini), "Lifelines" (Magne & Tini), "Scoundrel Days", "Cry Wolf", "Mythomania", "Hunting High And Low", "The Sun Always Shines On TV", "Foot Of The Mountain", "Under The Makeup", "The Living Dayllights" and &Quot;Take On Me".
