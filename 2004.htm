2004 - getting into the swing of things

February 2004

13       Magne, Molvær and Pellicier perform two "Meltdown" shows in Lillehammer, Norway

March 2004

17       Magne performs live at "Carnegie Art Award", Kunstnernes Hus, Oslo, Norway (3 songs)

April 2004

The album "Earth Affair - Chapter One" is released in Norway, it features the song "Gildas Prayer" performed by Morten Harket which is also released as a four track promo single.

 

June 2004

4       a-ha perform on a floating platform in the harbour, Bergen, Norway

August 2004

6       Savoy release the promo only single "Whalebone" from their forthcoming album "Savoy" in Norway.
7       Savoy perform at RaumaRock Festival, Åndalsnes, Norway
13       Øya Festival, Oslo, Norway
30       Savoy perform "Empty of Feeling" (playback) and "Whalebone" (Paul with live vocals) and are interviewed on the Norwegian breakfast TV show "Frokost-TV" (NRK1). A release concert with five live songs takes place at Blå, Oslo, Norway

 

31       Savoy release their fifth studio album "Savoy" in Norway.
Interview and footage of Savoy from the release concert at Blå is broadcast on the Norwegian TV show "Absolutt Underholdning" (TV2).

21       The "Nordic Music Awards" take place in Norway. a-ha receive an honoury award. Morten and Sissel also present an award to Robbie Williams. Morten and Jan Omdahl talk about the new book on "Senkveld - med Thomas og Harald" (TV2). a-ha's new book "The Swing Of Things", a book on a-ha's career, written by Jan Omdahl is released in Norway. It includes interviews with the band members plus a unique CD "The Demo Tapes" with 12 early previously unreleased tracks.
