2013 - In Transit

January 2013

9      A new hotel "The Thief" opens in Oslo and has an Apparatjik themed suite.

February 2013

13      The book "In Transit" about Magne's activities as an artist is released by Forlaget Press.
27      Stian Andersen's photo exhibition "a-ha: The Photographs" have private view at The Strand Gallery in London.

May 2013

2 - 1 June      The exhibition "Norwegian Wood" of ten new woodcut prints by Magne F is held at the Paul Stolper Gallery in London. The theme is music by The Beatles. The exhibition opens with a private view on 2 May and Magne attends.
3      Apparatjik bring their Light Space Modulator to Copenhagen, Denmark and perform at Statens Museum for Kunst.
19-24      The 15th anniversary of the Gumball 3000 road rally starts in Copenhagen. Magne and Einar Skjerven (Skjerven Racing - Team 43) take part in the race, initially in a Ferrari and finish (from Warsaw) in a Lada 1300 S due to technical issues. The 3,000 rally passes through Stockholm, Helsinki, St Petersburg, Tallinn, Rega, Vilnius, Warsaw, Krakow, Vienna, Verona and Monaco.
25      Morten joins Pitbull and Christina Aguilera on stage at the Billboard Music Awards show to sing the refrain of "Take On Me" as part of the "Feel This Moment" song.

December 2013

An EP from Marius Beck ("The Voice") called "Majors and Minors" is released in Norway. The EP was produced by Magne Furuholmen and Martin Terefe.
