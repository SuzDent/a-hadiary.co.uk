2017 - MTV unplugged

June 2017

22       a-ha - Øygardshallen, Giske, Norway. a-ha perform an exclusive acoustic set for MTV Unplugged in front of an audience of 250 people on the tiny island of Giske close to the city of Ålesund in Norway.
23       a-ha - Øygardshallen, Giske, Norway. The second of two acouostic performances for MTV Unplugged take place on the island of Giske. Alison Moyet is supposed to be one of the special guests at the show but is unable to stay until the evening. Instead, she performs "Summer Moved On" with a-ha in front of organisers of the event during the afternoon.

September 2017

17       a-ha - Armani event, The Tobacco Dock, London, UK (4 songs, electric)

October 2017

6       a-ha release an acoustic album "MTV Unplugged Summer Solistice&quoit; in Europe recorded on 22 and 23 June 2017. On the same date, the album "Acoustic Hits MTV Unplugged" was also released which includes a subset of individual tracks from the Summer Solistice show.
19       a-ha - BBC charity concert, The SSE Arena, London, UK. a-ha perform "Take On Me" acoustically.

December 2017

1       a-ha release a 2017 acoustic version of "Take On Me" as a download only single.
